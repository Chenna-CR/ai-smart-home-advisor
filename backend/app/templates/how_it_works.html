How It Works

AI Smart Home Advisor Workflow

The assistant turns a plain-language query into a ranked smart home shortlist using intent extraction, shopping search, weighted scoring, and explainable AI reasoning.

1
User Query
A natural-language request like "best smart AC for living room"
2
AI NLP (Intent Extraction)
Groq extracts product type, features, budget, and use case
3
Search (SerpAPI)
Live shopping results are fetched from the market
4
Ranking Algorithm
Feature match, rating, price fit, and reviews are weighted
5
AI Reasoning
Each recommendation gets a short explainable reason
6
Final Recommendation
The best product is highlighted for quick decision-making

Backend Explanation

The project uses FastAPI for the backend, Groq AI for language understanding, SerpAPI for live product search, and a heuristic ranking layer for final ordering.
